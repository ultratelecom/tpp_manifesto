Concept 1: Segmented Chapter Bar

Replace the simple progress bar with a segmented bar where each segment represents a TOC chapter. Segments are proportionally sized based on page count. Hover reveals chapter name.

üéØ Recommendation

Desktop: Use Concept 1 (Segmented Chapter Bar) - It's minimal, shows document structure, and provides quick chapter jumps on hover. Integrates nicely with the existing progress bar location.

Mobile: Combine Concept 4 (Smart Chapter Navigator) in the whitespace with Concept 5 (Expandable Drawer) for full chapter list. This gives:

  • Clear current chapter context
  • Easy prev/next chapter navigation
  • Full chapter grid when tapped
  • Uses existing whitespace = no obstruction
